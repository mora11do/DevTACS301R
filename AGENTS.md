# AGENTS

## Context
I previously took an agentic engineering class and now I am a TA.
I use this repo to experiment and research so the course can be improved in future semesters.

## Default Expectation
Assume my questions are research-focused unless I say otherwise.
This means my goals will tend to be focused more on learning and less on actually completing a specific task.
Creating a well-made program is not the end goal, usually.
More often than not, testing the capabilities of certain pre-made software is
more helpful than having you create something impressive from your own thinking
since students will be expected to recreate these tasks that I have.
